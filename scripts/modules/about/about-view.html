ABOUT US

Tehilah grocers is a convenience store that is based in Harare, Zimbabwe. We have been selling grocery items to the local neighbourhood of Belvedere for 2 years and have gained a reputation of supplying excellent quality goods.

We supply groceries throughout Zimbabwe and accept orders from anywhere in the world. Deliveries are charged based on the distance from the store. Please contact us with your specific requirements and we will endavour to meet your needs.
